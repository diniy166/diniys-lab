🔬 About Diniys Lab

Hello! My name is Dinaol Girma, founder of Diniys Lab. My mission is to inspire Ethiopian youth to embrace science, technology, engineering, and mathematics through exciting, accessible, and educational experiments.

What started as a passion project has grown into a community of more than 200,000 STEM enthusiasts on TikTok and 19,000+ followers on Instagram. Together, we're building a culture of curiosity, innovation, and scientific thinking.

To take Diniys Lab to the next level, I need support to acquire professional equipment, laboratory tools, safety gear, and materials for advanced STEM projects that can inspire thousands more young people across Ethiopia.
